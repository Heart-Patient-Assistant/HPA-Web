Vegan Spaghetti Bolognese

Method

1-Mash the tofu with potato masher, set aside then In a food processor pulse the carrot chunks into a grainy consistency, the pieces should be the size of rice grains.

2-Set that aside and do the same with mushrooms, careful not to over process it into a mash.

3-Chop up or grate garlic on microplane then Heat up olive oil in a saucepan, throw in the mushrooms and carrots.

4-Sauté it for a couple of minutes then add garlic, spices, salt and pepper and Sauté until fragrant then add tofu.

5-Now add soy sauce, tomato paste and molasses, mix well and sauté for few more minutes

6-Add canned or fresh chopped tomatoes, season it with salt and pepper and let it cook for 10, 15 minutes adjusting the consistency.

7-Add a little water if it’s too thick or let it reduce down if it’s too watery.

8-In the meantime cook spaghetti according to the instructions on the package. I love all my pasta aldente.

9-Once the sauce is done add nutritional yeast (optional) and chopped basil leaves, turn off the stove.

10-Drain cooked spaghetti, add half the sauce, mix well, serve and let the family dig in immediately.

11-Let the rest of the sauce cool down to room temperature, store it in an airtight container and freeze it up to 3 months

INGREDIENTS

  • 300g block of very firm tofu
  • 2 medium carrot
  • 200g mushroom
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cans of chopped tomatoes (or 5, 6 medium fresh tomatoes)
  • 1tbs tomato paste
  • 2tbs olive oil
  • 1tbs molasses
  • sea salt, pepper
  • 1tbsp soy sauce
  • 1tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2tsp nutmeg
  • 1tsp oregano
  • handfull of basil leaves
  • 1tbs nutritional yeast (optional)
  • half the pack of whole-wheat spaghetti 250g
