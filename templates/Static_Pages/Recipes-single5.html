Lentils With Herbs and Grilled Veggies

Method

1-Preheat the oven to 180C then Cut the aubergine, carrots and onions and tomatoes into cubes.

2-Put them into a large bowl, add minced garlic, thyme, sea salt, pepper and a spoon of olive oil and Mix well and spread it on a baking sheet.

3-Bake it for 20-30 minutes until slightly browned on the edges and the carrots are soft.

4-In a large bowl mix the cooked lentils with grilled vegetables, add the herbs and the remaining olive oil.

5-Season to taste with salt and pepper add a squeeze of lemon if you like.

6-Serve it with Hummus or tahini and warm bread

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups cooked dark lentils
  • 1 medium aubergine
  • 2 medium carrot
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 large white sweet onion
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2tbs olive oil
  • few springs of thyme
  • sea salt, pepper
  • loosely packed cup of mixed chopped herbs (dill, parsley, coriander, chives)
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • Hummus to serve
