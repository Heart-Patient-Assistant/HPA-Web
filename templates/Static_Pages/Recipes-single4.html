Roasted Baby Potato Summer Salad

Method

1-Preheat the oven to 200C or turn on the top grill in the oven and Boil the potatoes until cooked through in plenty of salted water.

2-Drain the potatoes cut them into halves. Drizzle with 1/2tbs of olive oil coating it well and spread it on a baking tray.

3-Bake or roast under the grill until slightly browned.

4-While the potatoes are cooking, slice the mushrooms, coat them with 1tsp of olive oil and roast under the grill for 3-5 minutes then Take them out and transfer into a large bowl.

5-Blanch the sugar snaps in plenty of water for one minute, drain and rinse it with ice cold water. Set aside to dry.

6-Slice the onions, cut the tomatoes in half , chop the herbs.

7-Add all the ingredients into a bowl with mushrooms, mix well and season with salt and pepper.

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g baby potatoes
  • 200g cherry tomatoes
  • 250g button mushrooms
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 100g sugar snap peas
  • 3 spring onions
  • 2tbs olive oil
  • 1/2cup chopped herbs (dill, parsley, chives
  • sea salt, pepper
